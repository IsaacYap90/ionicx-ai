## 2. COMPETITOR RESEARCH

### Competitor A: Mampu AI (mampuai.com) — Malaysia

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Positioning** | "Malaysia's Leading AI Agent Platform" |
| **Target** | Malaysian SMEs, especially sales-driven businesses |
| **Pricing** | From USD $99/month (~SGD $135/month). Subscription model. |
| **Key Hook** | "AI that handles objections like 'Can you sell cheaper?'" — addresses real sales pain. |
| **Social Proof** | "15x my sales from the first month" — specific, bold ROI claim. "Trusted by" with business testimonials. |
| **Channels** | WhatsApp Business API, multi-channel (web, social, WhatsApp) |
| **CRM** | Built-in CRM with lead scoring, reporting, analytics |
| **Strengths** | MYR pricing, Malay language AI, sales automation focus, appointment booking, team management |
| **Weaknesses** | No government tax incentive angle. Website is Wegic-built (template feel). Limited third-party reviews (not on G2/Capterra). |

**Key Takeaways:**
- Mampu leads with **sales outcomes** ("15x sales"), not features
- They solve specific pain: "handles price objections automatically"
- Monthly subscription model = lower barrier to entry than your one-time S$2,888

## 4. PRIORITIZED ACTION PLAN

### TIER 1: HIGHEST IMPACT (Do This Week)

#### 1.1 Add Bold ROI Claims to Hero Section

**Current hero subheadline:**
> "Your own AI employee that handles your website, answers customers 24/7, manages bookings, and never takes leave."

**Recommended change:**
> "Your own AI employee that answers customers 24/7, captures leads while you sleep, and costs as little as S$924 after the EIS tax deduction."

**Add a stats bar directly below the hero with outcome-focused numbers:**

| Current Stats | Recommended Stats |
|--------------|-------------------|
| 10 Projects Delivered | "10+" → "Industries Served" |
| 10 Industries Served | "24/7" → "Your AI Never Sleeps" |
| 400% EIS Tax Deduction | "68%" → "Average EIS Savings" |
| 24/7 AI Support | "2-3 Weeks" → "From Brief to Live" |

**Why:** Competitors lead with outcomes. Your stats currently describe activity, not results. Reframe to emphasize what the customer gets.

---

#### 1.2 Overhaul Testimonials Section — Make It Believable

**Current problem:** Testimonials read like they were written by the site owner. No photos, no links, no verification.

**Recommended actions:**

1. **Add real photos** of each client (or their business). Even a storefront photo adds credibility.

2. **Add measurable outcomes to each testimonial.** Replace:
   > "IonicX built our bakery website in 2 weeks. The AI chatbot handles 80% of our customer inquiries automatically."

   With:
   > "Before IonicX, I was spending 3 hours a day answering the same WhatsApp messages. Now the AI handles 80% of inquiries and I've gotten 47 new bookings in the first month alone. The website paid for itself in week 2." — **Sarah Chen, Sweet Bliss Bakery** *(link to demo site)*

3. **Add a "Results" metric to each testimonial card:**
   - Sweet Bliss Bakery: "47 new bookings in Month 1"
   - Iron Temple SG: "Effectively paid S$1,888 for a S$5,888 website"
   - Elite Properties: "3x more property inquiries via AI search"

4. **Add a video testimonial** — even a 30-second selfie video from one client changes everything. Film on iPhone. Authentic > polished.

#### 1.3 Add an EIS Tax Savings Banner — Site-Wide

**Recommended:** Add a sticky banner or announcement bar at the top of every page:

**Copy:**
> "SG Budget 2026: Get your AI website for as low as S$924 with the 400% EIS tax deduction → Learn More"

**Why:** This is your #1 differentiator and no competitor is using it. It should be impossible to miss. Currently it's buried in stats and the pricing page.

#### 1.4 Add a Risk Reversal / Guarantee

**Current state:** FAQ mentions "Satisfaction guarantee; revise mockup until happy" — but it's buried and vague.

**Why:** Competitors offer "free demos" and "setup in 5 minutes" — low risk entry. Your S$2,888 entry point needs a strong guarantee to de-risk the decision.
